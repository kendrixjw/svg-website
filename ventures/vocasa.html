What It Does

Describe a space out loud or sketch it roughly, and Vocasa drafts an editable, to-scale floor plan — turning natural language and rough ideas into precise layouts.

Who It’s For

Homeowners, renovators, designers, and builders who want to move from idea to accurate floor plan without wrestling with complex CAD software.
